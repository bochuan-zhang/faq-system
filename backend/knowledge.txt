## ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

### How do I create an account?
To create an account, visit our website and click the "Sign Up" button. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email address. Once verified, you can log in and start using our services. We also support single sign-on (SSO) for enterprise customers.

### How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email to create a new password. The reset link expires after 24 hours for security.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, you can change your email address in your account settings. Go to Profile > Account Settings > Email and enter your new email address. You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect. This helps ensure account security.

### How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to Account Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will permanently delete all your data. We recommend exporting your data first using the Data Export feature.

### What are the different account types?
We offer several account types: Free (1GB storage), Pro ($9/month, 10GB), Business ($25/user/month, 100GB), and Enterprise (custom pricing, unlimited storage). Each plan includes different features and storage limits.

## BILLING AND PAYMENTS

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.

### How do I update my billing information?
You can update your billing information in your account settings under Billing > Payment Methods. Add a new payment method or update existing card details. Your new payment method will be used for future charges. We'll notify you before charging your card.

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Go to Account Settings > Billing > Subscription and click "Cancel Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact our support team for a full refund. After 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Enterprise customers have custom refund policies.

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
To change your plan, go to Account Settings > Billing > Subscription and select "Change Plan." You can upgrade immediately or downgrade at the end of your current billing period. Plan changes take effect immediately for upgrades.

### Do you offer annual discounts?
Yes, we offer significant discounts for annual billing. Annual plans save you 20% compared to monthly billing. You can switch to annual billing at any time in your account settings under Billing.

## PRODUCT FEATURES

### How do I upload files?
To upload files, click the "Upload" button in the main interface. You can drag and drop files directly into the upload area or click to browse your computer. Supported file types include PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, images, and more. Maximum file size is 100MB per file.

### Can I share documents with others?
Yes, you can share documents by clicking the "Share" button on any document. Enter the email addresses of people you want to share with and set their permission level (view, edit, or admin). They'll receive an email invitation to access the document.

### How do I organize my documents?
You can organize documents by creating folders and subfolders. Click "New Folder" to create a folder, then drag and drop documents into it. You can also use tags to categorize documents for easier searching. Our advanced search helps you find documents quickly.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We use industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 standards.

### How does real-time collaboration work?
Real-time collaboration allows multiple users to edit the same document simultaneously. Changes are synchronized instantly, and you can see who's currently editing. We provide conflict resolution and version history to track all changes.

### What file types do you support?
We support most common file types including Microsoft Office documents (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), PDFs, images (JPG, PNG, GIF), text files, and more. Some file types can be previewed directly in the browser.

### How do I search for documents?
Use the search bar at the top of the interface to search by filename, content, or tags. Advanced search options allow you to filter by date, file type, or who shared the document with you. Search results update in real-time.

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Why can't I log in?
If you're having trouble logging in, try these steps: 1) Check that your email and password are correct, 2) Make sure Caps Lock is off, 3) Clear your browser cache and cookies, 4) Try a different browser. If the problem persists, contact support.

### Why are my files not uploading?
If files aren't uploading, check: 1) File size (max 100MB per file), 2) File type is supported, 3) Internet connection is stable, 4) Browser is up to date. If issues continue, try uploading smaller files or contact support.

### Why is the page loading slowly?
Slow loading can be caused by: 1) Large files being processed, 2) Slow internet connection, 3) Browser cache issues, 4) High server load. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

### Why can't I share documents?
If you can't share documents, check: 1) You have permission to share the document, 2) The email address is correct, 3) Your account is in good standing, 4) You haven't exceeded sharing limits. Contact support if issues persist.

### Why are my changes not saving?
If changes aren't saving, check: 1) Internet connection is stable, 2) You have edit permissions, 3) The document isn't locked by another user, 4) Your browser is up to date. Try refreshing the page.

When you reach your storage limit, you won't be able to upload new files until you either delete some existing files or upgrade your plan. You'll receive email notifications when you're approaching your limit.

Yes, individual files are limited to 100MB maximum. This ensures fast uploads and downloads for all users. If you need to work with larger files, consider splitting them or using our API for programmatic uploads.

### How many people can I share documents with?
Free accounts can share with up to 5 people. Pro accounts can share with up to 25 people, Business accounts with up to 100 people, and Enterprise accounts have unlimited sharing capabilities.
